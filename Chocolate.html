Finely handcrafted chocolates and cakes

Our wide assortment of milk, dark, and white chocolates and truffles will satisfy any taste bud.

Particular favorites are the orange peel, ginger, champagne truffles, caramel truffle and coffee truffles.

Chocolates are available in gift packs or loose.

Our after dinner slices, also known as chocolate wafers are a melt in the mouth experience that aren't available anywhere else.

Flavours include milk, milk coffee, caramel, dark, dark coffee, white chocolate and peppermint.

All our gift boxes have been specially handmade to ensure the best quality and selection.
